Man City held to 2-2 draw by West Ham United
 Manchester City suffered a setback to their title challenge as they were held by West Ham 2-2 at Upton Park on Saturday. Enner Valencia scored both goals for the home side while Sergio Aguero scored both goals for Manchester City. Manchester City moved to second place with the draw while West Ham remained in the sixth position in the table.
