4th SA-Eng Test Eng need 330 more runs to win 
 England finished the 4th day of their fourth test match against South Africa at 52/3. Set a target of 382 to win, England were rocked by two early blows from Kagiso Rabada when he dismissed Alex Hales and Alastair Cook. Earlier, gritty knocks of 96 and 78 from Hashim Amla and Temba Bavuma respectively helped SA declare at 248/5.
