Amla, Cook centuries propel SA to 329/5
 Former South African Test captain, Hashim Amla and debutant opener, Stephen Cook each scored centuries to help the Proteas finish day one of the fourth Test against England on 329/5 at the Centurion. England bowlers fought back in the last session of the day as they picked up four wickets after tea. Moeen Ali picked up two wickets for England.
