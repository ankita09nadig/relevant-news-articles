Mirza-Hingis seal quarters berth at Aus Open
 The top-ranked women's doubles team of Sania Mirza and Martina Hingis marched into the quarter-finals of the Australian Open after defeating Svetlana Kuznetsova and Roberta Vinci in straight sets 6-1, 6-3 on Monday. The Mirza-Hingis duo broke their opponents' serve five times, in comparison to the single break-of-serve by their opponents. The Indo-Swiss pair now has 33 consecutive wins.
