Mascherano gets one-year jail term for tax fraud
 Barcelona's Javier Mascherano has been given a one-year prison sentence and fined €815,000 approximately ₹6 crores on two counts of tax frauds of €600,000 in 2011 and over €968,000 in 2012. The Argentine could avoid prison time if he pays the money due and an added fine, apart from the €815,000 he has already been fined.
