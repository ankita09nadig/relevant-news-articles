Bopanna-Mergea crash out of Australian Open
 Rohan Bopanna and Florin Mergea crashed out of the men's doubles event at the 2016 Australian Open in the third round on Sunday. The Indo-Romanian pair went down against the duo of Treat Huey and Max Mirnyi 4-6, 3-6 in straight sets. Bopanna and Mergea committed a total of 23 unforced errors in the match, eight more than their opponents.
