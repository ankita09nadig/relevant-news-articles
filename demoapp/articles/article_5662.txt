Chanderpaul retires from international cricket
 West Indies cricketing legend, Shivnarine Chanderpaul on Saturday retired from all forms of international cricket. Chanderpaul last played for West Indies in May 2015 in the home Test series against England. The 41-year-old scored 20988 runs in 454 international matches at an average of 45.72 with 41 centuries and 125 half-centuries to his name.
