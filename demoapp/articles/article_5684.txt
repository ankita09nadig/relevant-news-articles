Aus Open Serena, Sharapova progress to round 3
 World Number one Serena Williams won her second round match against Taiwanese opponent, Hsieh Su-Wei 6-1, 6-2 in straight sets to progress through to the third round of the Australian Open on Wednesday. Fifth-seeded Maria Sharapova also won her second round match against Belarusian Aliaksandra Sasnovich 6-2, 6-1 in straight sets and made it to the next round.
