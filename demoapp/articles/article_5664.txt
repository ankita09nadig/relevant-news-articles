Kejriwal may be 1st Indian CM at Madame Tussauds
 According to The Indian Express, the India partner of Madame Tussauds has asked AAP leader Arvind Kejriwal “to be an esteemed waxwork figure” at their upcoming museum in Delhi. Kejriwal will be the first Indian Chief Minister to have a statue at the Madame Tussauds wax museum if he agrees. The museum is expected to open in Delhi next year.
