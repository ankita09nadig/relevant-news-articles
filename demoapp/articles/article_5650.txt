Messi's winner helps Barcelona secure 2-1 win
 Barcelona clinched a hard-fought 2-1 win over Malaga on Saturday to sit top of the La Liga table. The Catalan giants took the lead in the second minute after Munir El Haddadi scored off an assist from Luis Suarez. Juanpi equalised for Malaga in the 32nd minute but Lionel Messi popped up with the winner early in the second half.
