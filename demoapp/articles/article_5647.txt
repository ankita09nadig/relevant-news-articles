PV Sindhu wins gold in Malaysia Masters
 Indian shuttler PV Sindhu won the gold medal in the Malaysian Masters on Sunday after defeating Kirsty Gilmour of Scotland in the finals. The third-seeded Indian defeated the unseeded player 21-15, 21-9 in a match that lasted around half hour. With this, Sindhu clinched her first title of 2016, second Malaysian Grand Prix gold and fifth career Grand Prix gold.
