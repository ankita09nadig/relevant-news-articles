Bayern beat Atlético 1-0, finish 2nd in CL group stage
 Bayern Munich registered a 1-0 victory over Atlético Madrid in the final group stage match of the UEFA Champions League on Tuesday. Robert Lewandowski scored the lone goal of the match, his fifth in the CL this season, netting the ball with a 28th-minute free kick from 18 yards. This was Bayern's record 15th straight Champions League home victory.
