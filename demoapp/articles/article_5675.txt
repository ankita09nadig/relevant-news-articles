Hewitt ends singles tennis career with loss
 Former World number one Lleyton Hewitt ended his 18-year singles tennis career as he bowed out of the Australian Open with a 2-6, 4-6, 4-6 loss to Spaniard David Ferrer in the 2nd round. He had earlier announced that he would retire following the 2016 Australian Open. He won three Grand Slams and two Davis Cup titles during his career.
