Sharapova and Serena progress to the 4th round
 Fifth-seeded Maria Sharapova made it to the fourth round of the Australian Open as she beat American player Lauren Davis 6-1, 6-7, 6-0. Sharapova will now face Swiss youngster, Belinda Bencic in the fourth round. Elsewhere, top-seeded Serena Williams also made it to the fourth round after defeating Russia's Daria Kasatkina 6-1, 6-1 in the third round.
