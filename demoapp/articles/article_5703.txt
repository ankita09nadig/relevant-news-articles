Four Portuguese in team of Euro 2016
 UEFA announced the Euro 2016 team of the tournament on Monday. The team features four Portuguese footballers, including Cristiano Ronaldo, Pepe, Raphael Guerreiro and goalkeeper Rui Patricio. The tournament's top-scorer, Antoine Griezmann, is included alongside compatriot Dimitri Payet. Germany's Toni Kroos, Joshua Kimmich, and Jerome Boateng feature alongside Wales' Aaron Ramsey and Joe Allen.
