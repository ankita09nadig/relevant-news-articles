NZ win T20I series against Pakistan
 New Zealand on Friday won the three-match T20I series against Pakistan 2-1 after winning the third T20I by 95 runs in Wellington on Friday. Corey Anderson was named the man of the match as his 82 off 42 balls helped the Kiwis reach 196/5 in 20 overs. Grant Elliott and Adam Milne picked up three wickets each for New Zealand.
