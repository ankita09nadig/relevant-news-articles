Sharapova, Serena to faceoff in Aus Open QF
 Maria Sharapova and Serena Williams progressed to the quarter-finals of the Australian Open where they will face each other in a replay of last year's final. Sharapova defeated Belinda Bencic 7-5, 7-5 in her 4th round match that lasted more than 2 hours. Williams on the other hand, comfortably defeated Margarita Gasparyan 6-2, 6-1 in her 4th round encounter.
