Australia take 4-0 lead against India
 Australia beat India by 25 runs in the fourth ODI in Canberra to take a 4-0 lead in the five-match series. Centuries from Virat Kohli and Shikhar Dhawan went in vain as India lost their nine wickets for 46 runs. Notably, Kohli became the fastest and youngest to score 25 ODI centuries, as he achieved the feat in 162 innings.
