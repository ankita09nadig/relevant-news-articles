Djokovic wins Kyrgios knocked out of Aus Open
 Reigning champion, Novak Djokovic won his third round match at the Australian Open against Italy's Andreas Seppi 6-1, 7-5, 7-6 in straight sets. Djokovic will now face Frenchman Gilles Simon in the fourth round. Meanwhile, Nick Kyrgios from Australia was knocked out of the tournament by Czech player Tomáš Berdych 6-3, 6-4, 1-6, 6-4.
