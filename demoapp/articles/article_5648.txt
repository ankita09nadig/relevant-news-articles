Leicester crush Stoke 3-0 to reclaim top spot
 Goals from Daniel Drinkwater, Jamie Vardy and Jose Leonardo Ulloa gave Leicester City a 3-0 win over Stoke City on Saturday and helped them go top of the Premier League table. Drinkwater's deflected shot gave Leicester the lead, before his precise through ball helped Vardy score his first goal in eight matches. Mahrez later assisted Ulloa to seal the win.
