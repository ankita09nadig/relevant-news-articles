New Zealand beat Pakistan by 70 runs in 1st ODI
 New Zealand defeated Pakistan by 70 runs in the first ODI in Wellington on Monday. Batting first, the Kiwis scored 280/8 in their 50 overs, with Henry Nicholls scoring 82 off 111 balls. In reply, Pakistan's innings folded at 210, with Babar Azam playing a fighting knock of 62. Trent Boult was the wrecker-in-chief for the Kiwis, taking 4 wickets.
