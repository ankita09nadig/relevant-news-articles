FA Cup Tottenham defeat Leicester 2-0 in replay
 Tottenham defeated Leicester City 2-0 in their FA Cup third round replay at the King Power stadium. The first leg had ended in a 2-2 draw. Heung-min Son gave Tottenham a first half lead and then provided the through ball for Chadli to convert in the second half. Spurs will now face Colchester in the fourth round on 30th January.
