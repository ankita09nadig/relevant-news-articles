Federer first man to win 300 Grand Slam matches
 Swiss legend Roger Federer on Friday became the first male tennis player to win 300 Grand Slam matches. He achieved this feat after defeating Bulgarian Grigor Dimitrov 6-4, 3-6, 6-1, 6-4 in the third round of the Australian Open. The only other tennis player to have won 300 Grand Slam matches is Martina Navratilova, with 306 total wins.
