Roger Federer reaches Aus Open quarterfinal
 World number three Roger Federer advanced-won his fourth round match against Belgian player David Goffin on Sunday, to advance to the quarterfinals of the Australian Open. Federer beat Goffin 6-2, 6-1, 6-4 to progress through to the next round. The Swiss number one will face Czech Republic player Tomas Berdych in the quarterfinals.
