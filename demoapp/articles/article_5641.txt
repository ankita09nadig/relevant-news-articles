4th SA-Eng Test SA reach 42/1 on Day 3
 Bad light ended day three of the fourth Test between South Africa and England at the Centurion early, as the hosts reached 42/1 in their second innings. First-innings centurions, Hashim Amla and Stephen Cook were at the crease as SA led by 175 runs. Kagiso Rabada's five-wicket haul helped SA bowl out England for 342 in the first innings.
