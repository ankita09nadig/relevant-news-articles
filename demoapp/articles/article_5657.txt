Sindhu enters final of Malaysian Grand Prix Gold
 Indian shuttler PV Sindhu reached the final of the 2016 Malaysian Grand Prix Gold after a hard-fought 21-19, 12-21, 21-10 win over Sung Ji-Hyun in the semi-finals on Saturday. The 20-year-old Indian took the first game, lost a tough 2nd game to Sung before eventually clinching the tie in 3 games, winning a one-sided 3rd game 21-10.
