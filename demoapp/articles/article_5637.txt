Real Madrid held to 1-1 draw by Real Betis
 Real Madrid failed to continue their winning streak under Zinedine Zidane as they were held to a 1-1 draw by Real Betis in La Liga on Sunday. Alvaro Cejudo's volley into the top corner gave Betis an early lead. Later, Karim Benzema leveled the game in the 71st-minute. Madrid is currently placed third, four points behind Barcelona and Atletico Madrid.
