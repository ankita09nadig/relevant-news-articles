Kidambi Srikanth loses in Malaysian GPG semis
 Indian Men's number one, Kidambi Srikanth was knocked out of the semi-final of the Malaysian GPG by Malaysia's Iskandar Zainuddin on Saturday. Srikanth lost in straight games against the local player 25-27, 9-21. The first game was a close encounter as the players had to play out 6 deuce points before Zainuddin won the game.
