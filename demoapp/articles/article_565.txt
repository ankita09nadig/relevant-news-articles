2 soldiers martyred as terrorists attack Army in Arunachal
 At least two soldiers on Sunday martyred in an encounter with NSCNK terrorists near Jairampur in Changlang district of Arunachal Pradesh. The encounter is underway between Assam Rifle regiment and NSCNK terrorists, said DGP Mukesh Sahay. Further details are awaited. In December last year, two Assam Rifles soldiers were martyred during an ambush by suspected NSCNK militants.
