Rohit Sharma vaults into top 10 of ODI Rankings
 Indian opener Rohit Sharma rocketed into the top ten of ODI Rankings for batsmen after being named man-of-the-series for scoring 441 runs against Australia. Three Indian batsmen - Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma and Shikhar Dhawan - made into the top ten. Captain MS Dhoni was 13th, slipping out of the top ten for the first time in seven years.
