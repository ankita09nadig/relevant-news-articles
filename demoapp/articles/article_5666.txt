Aus Open Bopanna advances Bhupathi crashes out
 The pair of Rohan Bopanna and Florin Mergea won their second round men's doubles match at the Australian Open, defeating the Czech pair of Lukáš Dlouhý and Jiří Veselý 6-3, 6-2. However, the other Indian in action, Mahesh Bhupathi and his partner Gilles Müller crashed out of the tournament after losing to the Bryan brothers Bob and Mike 3-6, 2-6.
