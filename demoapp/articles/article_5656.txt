EPL Liverpool clinch thrilling 5-4 win 
 Liverpool won 5-4 against Norwich City in the Premier League on Saturday. The visitors led through Roberto Firmino, but goals from Dieumerci Mbokani and Steven Naismith gave Norwich the advantage. Wes Hoolahan doubled Norwich's advantage, but Liverpool hit back through 3 quickfire goals from Jordan Henderson, Firmino and James Milner. After Bassong equalised, Adam Lallana got the winner late on.
