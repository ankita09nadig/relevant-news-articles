Female Indian football referee qualified by FIFA
 Rupa Devi on Tuesday qualified to officiate international football matches conducted by FIFA. The 24-year-old referee from Dindigul, Tamil Nadu, is a teacher at the Madurai Kamaraj University, where she used to play football for the college team. She has refereeing experience at state and national level matches as well as at the AFC Under-14 Football Festival in 2013.
